VinUniversity TikTok Analytics Dashboard

This internal dashboard helps authorized VinUniversity staff monitor TikTok profile, video, follower, and engagement data for reporting and analytics.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

Purpose

The dashboard is used for internal communications and marketing analysis of the official VinUniversity TikTok presence.

Data Access

The service uses platform-authorized TikTok APIs to retrieve account profile, video, follower, and engagement information after permission is granted by an authorized account holder.
