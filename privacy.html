Privacy Policy

VinUniversity TikTok Analytics Dashboard

Last updated: July 7, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the VinUniversity TikTok Analytics Dashboard collects, uses, and stores information obtained through authorized TikTok APIs for internal analytics and reporting.

2. Information We Process

Depending on the permissions granted, the dashboard may process the following data:

  • TikTok account profile information
  • Follower counts and profile statistics
  • Video metadata and performance metrics
  • Video engagement metrics such as views, likes, comments, and shares
  • Authentication tokens required to access authorized API endpoints

3. How We Use Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Monitor TikTok performance for VinUniversity
  • Prepare internal communications and marketing reports
  • Analyze audience growth and content engagement trends
  • Maintain and improve internal reporting workflows

4. Data Sharing

Data accessed through the dashboard is intended for authorized internal use only. We do not sell personal information or disclose it to unrelated third parties except where required by law or necessary for secure system operation.

5. Data Retention and Security

We retain data only for as long as reasonably necessary for analytics, reporting, operational continuity, and compliance. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect stored data and access credentials.

6. Third-Party Platforms

This dashboard relies on TikTok APIs and may be subject to the policies and platform terms of TikTok. Data access is limited by permissions granted by the authorized TikTok account.

7. Contact

For official university information, please visit vinuni.edu.vn.
